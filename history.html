History of Premier Excellence

Premier Excellence is a house-building business that was established in 2023 in Gaborone,
Botswana, in response to the growing demand for quality
and affordable housing in the area.

In its early stages, Premier Excellence began with small-scale construction activities,
including home repairs, renovations, and minor extensions. The business focused on gaining experience
and building strong supplier relationships.

Through dedication to quality work, Premier Excellence earned client trust.
Word-of-mouth helped the business grow and secure more projects.

As demand increased, the company expanded into full house construction,
hiring skilled workers and improving equipment.

Today, Premier Excellence is a reliable construction company in Gaborone, focused on quality,
customer satisfaction, and future growth across Botswana.
