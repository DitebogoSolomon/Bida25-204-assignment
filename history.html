Our Story

Our History

From humble beginnings to a trusted name in Gaborone's construction industry.

How We Got Here
2023 — Founded
Established in Gaborone
Premier Excellence was established in 2023 in Gaborone, Botswana, in response to the growing demand for quality and affordable housing in the area.
Early Stage
Small-Scale Beginnings
In its early stages, Premier Excellence began with small-scale construction activities, including home repairs, renovations, and minor extensions. The business focused on gaining experience and building strong supplier relationships.
Growth
Earning Trust
Through dedication to quality work, Premier Excellence earned client trust. Word-of-mouth helped the business grow and secure more projects across Gaborone.
Expansion
Full House Construction
As demand increased, the company expanded into full house construction, hiring skilled workers and improving equipment to handle larger and more complex projects.
Today
A Reliable Name in Botswana
Today, Premier Excellence is a reliable construction company in Gaborone, focused on quality, customer satisfaction, and future growth across Botswana.
